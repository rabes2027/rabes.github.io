Sonnet Writing: Conflicts in Love

A Ballad of Conflicts in Love (A Sonnet by Raphael Abes)

Inspired by the song "Rewrite The Stars" by Zendaya and Zac Efron from the movie "The Greatest Showman", this Sonnet encompasses the theme of conflicts in love. After reading a short piece of art, you'll be inside the view and perspective of 2 lovers who's massive differences forbid them to be together. While overall a sad concept, the message it conveys is highly meaningful and emotional.

Torn Between Two Hearts (A Sonnet by Raphael Abes)

Based on the song "Someone Like You" by Adele, Torn Between Two Hearts brings you to the world of a person who still hasn't gotten over a previous heartbreak. Constantly bringing back thoughts of the past which continues to hurt them even more. They soon get an imaginary figure of their ex-lover, which results in a moment of happiness, yet quickly turns into eternal sadness. A deep message that's sure to strike the emotions of readers.
